We do the Magic.

We're here to make life easier.

UnifiedDashboard is more than just a dashboard. A place to imagine... Developed as Dimensional Internal Tool in 2009. Back in the day, it's a real struggle to keep track of projects that we're working on, managing devices, as well onboarding. That's what UnifiedDashboard is, it's a place to manage things in a single, consolidated place.

UnifiedDashboard became part of Dimensional Open Source Software Libraries (OSSL) and open to public beta since 2012. And released as public stable release in 2014, alongside with Dashboard Mainnets.

In 2016, UnifiedDashboard reaches 200K install base (with exception for enterprise users). In 2019, Dimensional renamed it's Dashboard Mainnets to Brotherhood One.

In 2023, UnifiedDashboard reaches 1 million user base. This is also where UnifiedDashboard is heavily used for remote and hybrid work, especially during COVID-19 pandemic.
